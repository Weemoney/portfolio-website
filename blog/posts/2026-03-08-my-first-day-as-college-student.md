**#Crash in The Dark**

4am. First day of university. Streets dark, lights dead.

I was riding to campus for new student orientation, nervous, trying to look like I belonged. 

60km/hour.. then — BOOM. I hit something. Except there was nobody there... until suddenly there was.

A man. Full black outfit. Appearing out of nowhere like a final boss.

He said I hit him.

I was bleeding. Scared. I pulled out my own disinfectant — yes I carried disinfectant — and this man, like some kind of angel-villain, took it from me and started treating my wounds himself.

While cleaning my cuts, he gave me a biology lecture. About cell regeneration. About how the body heals itself.
He knew I was a biology student before I told him.
I still don't know who he was.
But somehow, that's how university started for me.
