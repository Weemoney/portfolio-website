**2. "Do you agree with the rating system portrayed in the episode? Please explain your answer briefly."**

I disagree with the rating system shown in 'Nosedive'. While it aims to encourage politeness, it ultimately creates a superficial society where people lose their authenticity out of fear of social rejection. This system transforms human interaction into a cold transaction and leads to systemic discrimination, where a person’s basic rights and social mobility are unfairly dictated by a fluctuating numerical score.
