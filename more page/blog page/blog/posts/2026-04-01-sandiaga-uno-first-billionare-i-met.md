# Who rules the money?: Sick body from my sick story.

Along the way, I met black guy from Jayapura who study in UM as Economic Education student. We attend the events together, and long story short, when The Man of The Event stepped in. I got heat, dizzy, and feel undelicious, I just realize I got sick. Sick Knowledge with sick head, sick body also.
